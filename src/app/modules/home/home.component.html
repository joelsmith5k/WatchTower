The named WatchTower is derived from Latin 'Specula', a root of the modern Speculate; to form a theory or conjecture about a subject without firm evidence.

Speculation has fascinated and defined modern societies - an estimated $7.5 trillion exchanges hands in speculative markets everyday. It provides a chance to predict the future, and build and lose fortunes in the process.

The goal of WatchTower Sports is to assist with informed speculation based on statistics, trends, and predicitive analytics of unique, unexplored, or neglected sports betting markets. WatchTower is built with an Angular frontend and ASP.NET Core backend.
